Risk Framework
Guardrails
These are not preferences. They are covenantal commitments. A broken guardrail triggers a mandatory pause — not a fight.
🔑 Nicole — Gatekeeper Rules
Gate before entry
Every position above the fast-lane threshold requires Nicole's explicit approval before Jason enters. Silence is not approval.
Veto no justification
Nicole can say no or "size it down" without winning a debate. The gate is about risk management, not market opinion.
FOMO override
"This one's different", "just this once", and urgency pressure are explicitly not valid reasons to bypass the gate.

Leverage policy
Monthly loss limit

⟡ Both — Shared Commitments
Monthly review
First Sunday of every month. 30 minutes minimum. Non-negotiable.
48-hour rule
Any loss, windfall, new debt, or rule break is communicated within 48 hours. No financial secrets.
Pool is sacred
Pool capital is never used for emergencies, lifestyle, or "I'll pay it back." Investment capital only.
Exit clause
If the relationship ends, pool is liquidated. Each party receives their proportional contribution ± their share of realised P&L.
No blame at reviews
Reviews are for recalibration, not prosecution. Rule breaks are data, not verdicts.
